v2.0.9 — Hotfix for the photo viewer.

v2.0.8 shipped with a path-resolution bug that prevented the dedicated viewer window from loading its UI in installed builds (it worked fine in development). Photos and videos opened from Search & Discovery, Memories, and Albums weren't displaying. v2.0.9 restores the viewer.

No data was at risk — the viewer just refused to render. This update will install automatically the next time you close PDR.
