6. MOTION & TRANSITIONS
Core Animation Easing
Primary ease function: cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94)

Smooth, slightly springy (bouncy without being playful)
Used for card hovers, button presses, major interactions
Timing
Fast interactions: 0.18s–0.2s (color changes, opacity)
Medium interactions: 0.4s (card lift, button scale, border transitions)
Slow/fade transitions: 0.55s (opacity fade-in for rotator text)
Specific Motions
Button hover: Lift 2px + scale 1.02 over 0.4s
Card hover: Lift 2–4px + scale 1.02, border color shift
Link underline: Gradient underline (lavender→gold) that expands on hover
Hero rotator text: Fade in/out over 0.55s (subtle, contemplative)
No jarring animations
Subtle, professional feel
Used to guide focus, not distract
Mostly micro-interactions on hover/click
7. OVERALL TONE & KEYWORDS
Visual Tone:

Premium — refined colour palette, generous spacing, subtle shadows
Calm & Reassuring — soft lavender, smooth transitions, no aggressive reds/oranges
Trustworthy — clean typography, clear hierarchy, straightforward layout
Sophisticated — minimal without being stark, purposeful use of colour and space
Inviting — rounded buttons, soft shadows, approachable spacing
Design Philosophy:

"Generous spacing" over "compact density"
"Subtle depth" over "flat design"
"System fonts" over "custom typefaces" (accessibility, speed)
"Micro-interactions" over "obvious animations"
"Lavender-as-primary" — calming, premium, distinctive
8. RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR
Desktop (1120px+): Full two-column layouts, three-column card grids, generous gaps
Tablet (860px–1119px): Two-column grids, stacked sections
Mobile (<540px): Single column, full-width cards, reduced padding (20px)
Anchor scroll margin: calc(70px + 16px) (respects sticky header height)
