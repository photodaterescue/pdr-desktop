v2.0.14 — Responsiveness across the board, HEVC video playback, and drilldown sidebar navigation.

This release focuses on speed across PDR plus a handful of features the v2.0.13 dogfood surfaced as obvious gaps.

Speed and responsiveness:

Analysis now runs in its own background process. Adding a source used to lock the rest of PDR up while the analysis chewed through your photos — drag the window and the title bar went white, scroll the grid and thumbnails sat blank. v2.0.14 moves the heavy work into a separate background process so PDR stays smooth while it's happening.

Year drilldown in Memories — By Date now opens instantly even with thousands of photos. The grid renders only the tiles currently on screen and brings in more as you scroll, instead of mounting every photo on entry. Scrolling to mid-year no longer leaves tiles as empty placeholders while the background thumb-load crawls in order from January — PDR loads whatever's on your screen first.

The PDR Viewer opens the image right away instead of waiting fifteen seconds for the filmstrip to mount. Opening a photo from a year drilldown is now near-instant; the previous URL-based approach was passing every photo's file path through the viewer's address bar, and PDR now does that via a direct internal call instead.

HEVC video playback (OnePlus, Samsung, iPhone Motion Photos):

The short .mp4 clips that sit alongside iPhone Live Photos and Samsung / OnePlus / Pixel Motion Photos used to show as blank thumbnails and play sound-only over a black frame. They now thumbnail correctly and play in the viewer with picture + sound. Behind the scenes, PDR transcodes HEVC clips to H.264 on first play (cached for instant subsequent plays).

Drilldown sidebar navigation:

A new left-column sidebar on Memories — By Date matches the timeline view's pattern at every depth: timeline shows years, year drilldown shows months (Jan, Feb, Mar…), month drilldown shows days (1, 2, 5, 12…). One click jumps straight to that month or day, and the current location is highlighted. Two floating circular arrow buttons at the bottom-right of the drilldown give you D (day-step) and (on year drilldowns) M (month-step) — useful for jumping a month at a time without scrolling through 30 days.

The drilldown now sorts newest-first to match the timeline view above it and what every premium photo app does (Apple Photos, Google Photos, Lightroom). December at the top of a year, latest photo of the day first.

Captioned-only filter (mirrored from Albums):

The "Captioned only" gold chip introduced in Albums last release is now also on the By Date drilldown header and on Search & Discovery, so you can narrow any view to just the photos with captions.

Rotation:

Rotating a photo in the viewer now updates that photo's thumbnail everywhere immediately — Memories grid, Albums tiles, Search & Discovery results, viewer filmstrip. Previously the thumbnail kept showing the pre-rotation version until you navigated away.

Robustness:

Photo thumbnails for paths over Windows' classic 260-character limit now extract correctly (the previous bug affected every video thumbnail regardless of length, not just long paths).

This update will install automatically the next time you close PDR.
