v2.0.8 — Albums arrive in Memories. PDR now organises your photos into albums alongside the chronological timeline, with automatic import of every Google Photos album (and their original filenames + captions) from a Takeout. Drag albums between folders, export any album as a Parallel Library in one click, and add to albums from anywhere — Search & Discovery, Memories By Date, or directly inside an empty album.

Search & Discovery also gets a major polish pass: contextual counts on every filter dropdown (tick Iceland and the City list narrows itself), a two-tier "gate" / "narrower" filter model with an amber banner when the gates are emptied, and a calmer results bar with the action buttons tucked into a "More actions" menu.

Plus: clearer message when your Library Drive isn't reachable (no more silent fallback to your system drive); inline filter chips next to Edit Dates; Workspace-style zoom controls inside S&D and Albums; a "Back to my album" pill in the title bar when you've stepped out to pick photos; and Undo / Redo for album moves (Ctrl-Z, Ctrl-Y).

This update will install automatically the next time you close PDR.
