Instruction: Fix Complete colour alignment (no new colours)

Goal:
The Fix Complete modal must visually match the Analysis complete badge and the rest of the Workspace success states.
Do not introduce new greens or stronger emphasis than already exists in the Workspace.

✅ Authoritative source of truth

Use the Analysis complete badge as the single source of truth for success green.

That means:

Emerald 400–500 range only

No emerald-600+

No dark-mode-only greens that don’t exist elsewhere

🎨 Exact colour changes to make
1️⃣ Success circle (icon at top of Fix Complete)

Replace:

bg-emerald-50

border-emerald-200

bg-emerald-900/30 (dark)

With:

bg-emerald-100
border-emerald-300
text-emerald-500


Dark mode:

dark:bg-emerald-900/20
dark:border-emerald-600
dark:text-emerald-400


👉 This matches the subtle, calm success language already used in Workspace.

2️⃣ Confirmed / Recovered / Marked numbers

Replace all:

text-emerald-600

text-emerald-400 (dark)

With:

text-emerald-500
dark:text-emerald-400


No exceptions.

3️⃣ “View Report” primary button (this is the biggest offender)

Replace entirely:

bg-emerald-600
hover:bg-emerald-500


With:

bg-emerald-500
hover:bg-emerald-400
text-white


Dark mode:

dark:bg-emerald-600
dark:hover:bg-emerald-500


👉 This keeps it primary without overpowering the Workspace.

4️⃣ Remove any unique emerald tokens from Fix Complete

Explicitly forbidden in Fix Complete UI:

emerald-600

emerald-700

emerald-800

emerald-900 (except very low-opacity backgrounds as above)

If it doesn’t exist in the Workspace already, it should not exist in Fix Complete.

🧠 Design intent (important – include this)

The Fix Complete modal should feel calm, resolved, and consistent, not celebratory or louder than the Workspace.
Success is communicated through clarity, not saturation.

✅ Acceptance check (agent must confirm)

Ask the agent to confirm yes/no to each:

Fix Complete uses no emerald above 500

“View Report” button green matches Workspace success tone

Fix Complete no longer visually dominates the Workspace beneath it

If they can’t answer yes to all three, it’s not done.
